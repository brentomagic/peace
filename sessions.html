Workshop Lessons

Our workshop consists of three interactive lessons designed to build upon each other. Each lesson includes group discussions, practical exercises, and take-home activities that you can immediately apply in your relationship and family life. Lessons are designed to be engaging, supportive, and focused on real-world application.

Lesson 1: Building Strong Foundations in Preparation for Marital Success

Date: March 5, 2026 | Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

What we'll cover:

  • Understanding personal values and beliefs about marriage
  • Assessing emotional readiness for commitment
  • Identifying realistic expectations in relationships
  • Exploring qualities of healthy marriages
  • Reflecting on personal strengths and areas for growth

What to bring: Notebook and pen for taking notes and completing exercises

Lesson 2: Building Strong Communication and Conflict Management Skills for Healthy Marriage

Date: March 19, 2026 | Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

What we'll cover:

  • Learning active listening skills
  • Practicing healthy ways to express feelings and needs
  • Understanding common causes of conflict in marriage
  • Developing respectful conflict resolution strategies
  • Building trust through effective communication

What to bring: Completed take-home exercises from Lesson 1

Lesson 3: Financial and Relationship Preparedness: Building a Strong Future Together

Date: April 2, 2026 | Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

What we'll cover:

  • Understanding the role of finances in marriage
  • Setting shared financial goals
  • Creating simple budgeting strategies
  • Managing financial disagreements constructively
  • Planning for long-term relationship stability

What to bring: Completed take-home exercises from Lesson 2 (optional)
