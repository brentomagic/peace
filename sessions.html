Workshop Sessions

Our workshop consists of six interactive sessions designed to build upon each other. Each session includes group discussions, practical exercises, and take-home activities that you can immediately apply in your family life. Sessions are designed to be engaging, supportive, and focused on real-world application.

Session 1: Foundations of Family Communication

Date: March 5, 2026 | Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

What we'll cover:

  • Understanding different communication styles and their impact on relationships
  • Active listening techniques and how to validate family members' feelings
  • Identifying communication barriers and learning to overcome them
  • Practice exercises: role-playing scenarios and communication drills

What to bring: Notebook and pen for taking notes and completing exercises

Session 2: Building Trust and Emotional Safety

Date: March 12, 2026 | Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

What we'll cover:

  • Understanding the components of trust in family relationships
  • Creating emotional safety within your family unit
  • Strategies for rebuilding trust after conflicts or misunderstandings
  • Group activity: trust-building exercises and discussions

What to bring: Completed take-home exercises from Session 1

Session 3: Conflict Resolution and Problem-Solving

Date: March 19, 2026 | Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

What we'll cover:

  • Understanding the root causes of family conflicts
  • Step-by-step conflict resolution framework
  • Win-win problem-solving strategies
  • Practice session: working through real family scenarios

What to bring: A specific family challenge you'd like to work on (optional)

Session 4: Setting Boundaries and Respecting Differences

Date: March 26, 2026 | Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

What we'll cover:

  • Understanding healthy boundaries and why they matter
  • How to set and communicate boundaries respectfully
  • Respecting individual differences within your family
  • Balancing family unity with individual needs

What to bring: Reflection journal from previous sessions

Session 5: Strengthening Family Bonds and Creating Traditions

Date: April 2, 2026 | Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

What we'll cover:

  • The importance of quality time and shared experiences
  • Creating meaningful family traditions and rituals
  • Strategies for maintaining connection during busy times
  • Group activity: planning family activities together

What to bring: Ideas for family activities you'd like to explore

Session 6: Sustaining Growth and Moving Forward

Date: April 9, 2026 | Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

What we'll cover:

  • Reviewing key concepts and tools from all sessions
  • Creating a personal action plan for continued growth
  • Building a support network and finding ongoing resources
  • Celebration and reflection: sharing progress and next steps

What to bring: All workshop materials and your completed action plan draft
