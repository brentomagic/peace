Workshop Location

Community Center
Room A
72 Umuchichi road
Okpu-umobo, Aba, Abia state

Parking Information

Free parking is available in the Community Center parking lot. The parking lot is located on the east side of the building. Additional street parking is available on Main Street and adjacent side streets. Please arrive 10-15 minutes early to allow time for parking and finding the room.

Public Transportation

The Community Center is easily accessible by public transit. The Main Street bus stop (Route 5) is located directly in front of the building. The nearest subway station is Downtown Station, located 2 blocks north on Main Street.

Accessibility

The Community Center is fully wheelchair accessible. Room A is located on the ground floor with easy access from the main entrance. Accessible restrooms are available on the same floor. If you have specific accessibility needs, please contact us in advance so we can ensure your comfort.

What to Bring

  • Notebook and pen for taking notes
  • Water bottle (water fountains are available)
  • Comfortable clothing
  • An open mind and willingness to participate

Arrival Time: Please arrive 10-15 minutes before the session begins to allow time for check-in and settling in.

Directions to the Workshop Location

From Downtown Area

Head south on Main Street. The Community Center will be on your right side, approximately 0.5 miles from the city center. Look for the large brick building with the Community Center sign.

From Highway 101

Take Exit 15 toward Downtown. Turn right onto Main Street and continue for 1.2 miles. The Community Center will be on your left. The parking lot entrance is just before the building.

From the Airport

Take Airport Boulevard north for 3 miles, then turn left onto Main Street. Continue for 2 miles. The Community Center will be on your right. Estimated travel time: 15-20 minutes.
