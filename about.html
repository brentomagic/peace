About the Facilitator

Photo

Welcome!

My name is Peace Eleonu, and I am passionate about helping families build stronger, healthier relationships. With years of experience in family studies and relationship counseling, I have dedicated my career to understanding the dynamics that make families thrive.

Education & Training

I hold a degree in Family Studies and have completed extensive training in family therapy, communication strategies, and conflict resolution. My educational background provides me with a solid foundation in evidence-based practices, while my practical experience allows me to translate theory into actionable strategies that families can use in their daily lives.

Experience

Over the years, I have facilitated numerous workshops and counseling sessions, helping families navigate challenges ranging from communication breakdowns to major life transitions. I have worked with diverse families and understand that each family is unique, requiring personalized approaches and strategies.

My Approach

I believe that every family has the capacity for growth and positive change. My approach is collaborative, supportive, and practical. Rather than focusing solely on problems, I help families identify their strengths and build upon them. The workshop sessions are designed to be interactive, engaging, and immediately applicable to your family's situation.

What You Can Expect

In my workshops, you can expect a safe, non-judgmental environment where all participants are encouraged to share, learn, and grow. I combine evidence-based techniques with practical exercises that you can use right away. My goal is to empower you with tools and knowledge that will serve your family long after the workshop ends.

Why I Do This Work

I have seen firsthand how strong family relationships can transform lives. When families learn to communicate effectively, resolve conflicts constructively, and support each other through challenges, everyone benefits. I am committed to sharing what I've learned and helping families create the connections they deserve.
